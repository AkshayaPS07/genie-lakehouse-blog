# AI-Powered Business Intelligence in Minutes: Genie Data Rooms & Lakeview Dashboards

## Introduction:

Business Intelligence (BI) has evolved. Today, even non-technical users can get insights from their data thanks to AI-powered tools. Genie Data Rooms and Lakeview Dashboards enable simplified, natural-language driven BI experiences, helping business users make decisions faster.

### Section 1: What are Genie Data Rooms?

Genie Data Rooms are virtual spaces where data is curated, cleaned, and organized for analysis.

<u>Underlined Features:</u>

* AI-assisted data exploration

* Natural language queries: ask questions in plain English

* Secure collaboration across teams

<u>Underlined Benefit:</u> Users don’t need SQL or coding skills to extract insights.

### Section 2: What are Lakeview Dashboards?

Lakeview Dashboards turn curated data into interactive, easy-to-read visualizations.

<u>Underlined Features:</u>

* Drag-and-drop widgets

* AI-powered insights and suggestions

* Simplified KPIs for business users

<u>Underlined Benefit:</u> Decisions can be made quickly without relying on data analysts.

### Section 3: How Genie & Lakeview Work Together

Genie Data Room organizes and cleans your data.

You use natural language or simple drag-and-drop to create queries.

Lakeview Dashboard visualizes the insights immediately.

AI assistant can suggest trends, anomalies, and predictions.

### Section 4: Key Benefits for Business Users

* Faster decision-making

* Reduced dependency on IT/data teams

* More accurate insights with AI assistance

* Easier collaboration across teams

## Conclusion:

With tools like Genie Data Rooms and Lakeview Dashboards, AI-powered BI becomes accessible to all business users. No more waiting weeks for data reports—now insights are instant, visual, and actionable.
